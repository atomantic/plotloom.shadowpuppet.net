Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2, 2026

Data Storage

PlotLoom stores your book club data — your member name, clubs you belong to, books you propose, ratings, and notes — locally on your device and in your private iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit. Your display name and basic preferences are stored in UserDefaults on your device.

iCloud Sync & Sharing

PlotLoom uses Apple’s CloudKit to sync your clubs, proposals, ratings, and notes across your own iPhone, iPad, and Mac, and to share clubs with other members you invite via Apple’s built-in sharing (CKShare). Shared data is hosted in iCloud under each member’s own Apple ID. ShadowPuppet, LLC has no access to your iCloud data, your Apple ID, or the contents of your shared clubs.

For information about how Apple handles iCloud and CloudKit data, please refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

PlotLoom does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers operated by us. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • The titles, ratings, or notes you record about your reading

In-App Purchases

PlotLoom may offer optional in-app purchases in the future. If it does, all payment processing will be handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

Third-Party Services

PlotLoom does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs, pixels, or external scripts in the app.

No Account Required

PlotLoom does not require any account creation, login, or authentication on our servers. To use iCloud sync and sharing, you must be signed in to iCloud on your device through Apple’s standard system settings.

Children’s Privacy

PlotLoom does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for use by reading groups and book clubs of any age, but it does not include public social features or external communication mechanisms.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by removing yourself from clubs, deleting clubs you own, and uninstalling the app. Data stored in your private iCloud container can also be deleted through the iCloud system settings on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at plotloom@shadowpuppet.net. For general help with the app, visit Support.
