What it does

📖
Proposal Pool

Everyone in the club drops their picks into a shared pool. No more "what should we read next?" group threads.

🎲
The Loom Picks

When you’re ready, the loom weaves a random pick from the pool into "currently reading" and archives the previous one. Or run a ranked vote when the group wants a say.

⭐
Per-Member Ratings

Each member rates 1–5 stars. See group sentiment side by side, not flattened into a single anonymous average.

📝
Personal Notes

Capture your thoughts on each read in your own space — ready for the next meeting, no spoilers for anyone else.

☁️
iCloud Sync & Sharing

Your club, your books, your ratings — synced across devices and shared with members through iCloud. No accounts to set up.

📱
iPhone, iPad & Mac

Native SwiftUI app with the same density on every device. No ads, no third-party tracking, no subscription.
